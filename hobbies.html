How I relax

I love to spend most of my free time outdoors. Hiking, jogging, a stroll in the neighborhood, or just playing the guitar outside our house. I'm up for it. Such change of scenery brings relaxation especially for my weary eyes, what with all time spent staring into digital screens.

Sitting all throughout the day and facing our gadgets on close proximity to our face doing our jobs or studies is all well and good. However, just like solving problems in life, we should take a pause on our focus and look outside. Prolonged focus may cause nearsightedness - literally and figuratively speaking, causing us to miss out on something.

Doesn't matter if you're an introvert or an extrovert. Go outside, stretch those stiff muscles, relax your eyes and take some deep breathes. Don't cage yourself in your room thinking that is all life can offer.
